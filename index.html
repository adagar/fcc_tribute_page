A Timeline of Terry Pratchett's Life:

  • 1948 - Pratchett is born in Buckinghamshire, England to David and Eileen Pratchett
  • 1971 - Pratchett publishes his first story, The Carpet People
  • 1968 - Pratchett marries Lyn Purves
  • 1983 - The Colour of Magic, Pratchett's first Discworld novel, is published
  • 1998 - The Queen knights Sir Terry Pratchett for his contribution to awesomeness
  • 2007 - Pratchett announces that he has been diagnosed with early-onset Alzheimer's
  • 2015 - Pratchett passes away in his home.
  • 2015 - The Shepard's Crown, Pratchett's final novel is published, five months after his death
